Gautam Buddha Nagar District Religion Data - Hindu/Muslim

As per official census 2011 and population data 2025 of Gautam Buddha Nagar district, Hindu are majority in Gautam Buddha Nagar state. Total population of Gautam Buddha Nagar district is 1,648,115 as per census 2011. Hinduism constitutes 84.58% of Gautam Buddha Nagar population. Muslims are minority in Gautam Buddha Nagar state forming 13.08% of total population.
